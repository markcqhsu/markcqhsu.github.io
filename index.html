Stash Bag Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This privacy policy applies to StashBag (hereinafter referred to as "the App") regarding the collection, use, and protection of personal data. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Data Collection & Storage

The App is designed with a "local-first" approach. Inventory data you create (including collectibles, consumables, photos, prices, storage locations, tags, notes, etc.) is stored on your device's local storage and is not automatically uploaded to any cloud service or third-party server.

The App uses the following local storage methods:

  • SQLite database: stores inventory items and related fields
  • SharedPreferences: stores app preferences (e.g., dark mode, language, custom units)
  • Local file system: stores item photos

2. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services to provide specific features:

  • Rebrickable API: used for LEGO set search functionality. When you use this feature, your search query is sent to Rebrickable servers to retrieve set information. Please refer to Rebrickable's privacy policy for details on their data practices.
  • Camera / Photo Library: used to take or select item photos. Photos are stored locally only and are not uploaded externally.
  • Share feature: when you use the share feature, data is handled by the app you choose. The App does not control the privacy practices of those apps.

3. Purpose of Data Use

Data collected and stored by the App is used solely to:

  • Provide core features such as inventory management, shopping lists, and statistics
  • Display the interface according to your preferences (e.g., dark mode, language)
  • Support backup export / import functionality (sharing is entirely at your discretion)

4. Data Security & Deletion

Your data is stored on your local device. The App does not actively transmit data to external parties. If you uninstall the App, all local data will be removed.

You can export a JSON backup via "Settings > Data Management > Export Backup", or restore data via "Import Backup".

This privacy policy may be updated due to legal requirements or product changes. For significant changes, we will provide a notification or update notice within the App. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
