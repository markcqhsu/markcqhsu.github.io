Data collected and stored by the App is used solely to:

  • Provide core features such as inventory management, shopping lists, and statistics
  • Display the interface according to your preferences (e.g., dark mode, language)
  • Support backup export / import functionality (sharing is entirely at your discretion)

4. Data Security & Deletion

Your data is stored on your local device. The App does not actively transmit data to external parties. If you uninstall the App, all local data will be removed.

You can export a JSON backup via "Settings > Data Management > Export Backup", or restore data via "Import Backup".

5. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated due to legal requirements or product changes. For significant changes, we will provide a notification or update notice within the App. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store page or the developer's contact channel.

7. Data Source
